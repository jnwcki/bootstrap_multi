Welcome to my website on several of my favorite trails in the Killington, VT area. Here are several of my absolute favorites which I have personally guided guests on many times during the years which I worked at a guided outdoor adventure outfit in Killington. There are trails in the area for every skill level and activity.

Pico Peak

The slightly smaller sibling of Killington Peak, Vermont's second highest peak.

Deer Leap Mountain

A short hike suitable for beginner/intermediate hikers with breathtaking views.

Mt. Tom

Marsh Billings Rockefeller Nat'l Historic Park has miles of easy trail by the town of Woodstock.
